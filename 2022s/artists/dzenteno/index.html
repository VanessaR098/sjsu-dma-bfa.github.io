DreamScape
DAVID ZENTENO
ARTIST STATEMENT
DreamScape focuses on the timeline where the 2020 Covid pandemic started in the United States. It was a beautiful day; I was halfway through my college education. I was doing good on my classes, exploring campus was always a refreshing experience after completing my assignments, and hanging out with friends was great because it would distract me from the stress I had with my studies. Then, everything changed rapidly when the Covid pandemic was officially announced. Classes were no longer in person, you had to quarantine in your room, and most social places and events were canceled or closed. It was the most weirdest, darkest, loneliest, bizarre experience that lasted two years. This room represents the insanity of staying the room and being in isolation due to quarantine.
