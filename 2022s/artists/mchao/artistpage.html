如夢似幻 - Dreamlike
MEGAN CHAO
ARTIST STATEMENT
如夢似幻 - Dreamlike discusses the beauty in tradition as it showcases different Chinese mythological creatures as well as traditional Chinese clothing from the Han to Song Dynasty modeled by influencer and hanfu expert, MochiHanfu. My pieces are modeled using Blender and NomadSculpt and the photos are edited using Lightroom Classic and Photoshop. This work is meant to highlight the vast history and stories of my Chinese heritage.
ARTIST BIO
Megan Chao’s work focuses on encapsulating cultural appreciation and body positivity through fashion and art creation. She works with photography, 3D models, digital collages, and graphic layout design to create her vision. Her work takes inspiration from current trends and futuristic landscapes as well as traditional cultural elements such as Chinese clothing and styles. She aims to address cultural and political issues within both the fashion and current world, oping to both spread positivity and influence.
