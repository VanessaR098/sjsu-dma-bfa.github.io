ABOUT GALLERY

THE CADRE MEDIA LAB IS PROUD TO PRESENT REDISCOVERY, A HYBRID EXHIBITION THAT IS AVAILABLE TO VIEW IN NEW ART CITY
AND IN PERSON! REDISCOVERY FEATURES A WIDE RANGE OF WORK THAT MANAGES TO EXPAND ACROSS A GREAT VARIETY OF
MEDIUMS WITHIN DIGITAL MEDIA ART. THIS FINAL EXHIBITION SERVES AS THE PLATFORM IN WHICH ONE CAN EXPERIENCE
THE CULMINATION OF THE GRADUATING SENIORS’ WORK IN THE BACHELOR OF FINE ARTS DIGITAL MEDIA ART PROGRAM.

REDISCOVERY CAPTURES THE ESSENCE OF THE ARTIST AMIDST POLARIZING TIMES. IN A WORLD PERMEATED BY AGGREGATE CRISIS, MALLEABLE TRUTHS, AND DIGITAL ALGORITHMS,
ARTISTS IN THIS EXHIBITION ARE CHALLENGED TO DEFINE THEIR OWN STANDING IN THIS WORLD. WITHIN THE SEA OF LIMITATIONS IN THE FORM OF SOCIOECONOMICS, INEQUALITY
OF OPPORTUNITY, AND SOCIETAL NORMS, THE ARTIST IS TASKED WITH EMBODYING SUCH IMPEDIMENTS AS A MEANS OF OVERCOMING THEM. BY TRAVERSING THROUGH REDISCOVERY, ONE
CAN REACH THE PLACE WITHIN EACH ARTIST WHERE THIS FEAT OCCURS. THUS, A NEW CONCEIVABLE WORLD IS REFLECTED THROUGH THESE DISCOVERIES FOUND IN THIS CURATION OF WORKS.

FACULTY ADVISORS: GARY CRAIG HOBBS AND RHONDA HOLBERTON
